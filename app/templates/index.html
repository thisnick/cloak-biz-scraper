Connect your assistant

Add this server to ChatGPT or Claude once. Then just ask them to find listings — they do the rest through this server.

Server link {{ server_url }}
  1. Open Settings, and under Integrations click Plugins.
  2. Pick the MCPs tab at the top, then click Add Server.
  3. Give it a name, choose Streamable HTTP, and paste the server link above.
  4. Click Save.
  5. Click the Authenticate button that appears next to it, and enter your APP_SECRET.
  1. Open Settings → Connectors.
  2. Choose Add custom connector and paste the server link above.
  3. Click Connect, then sign in with your APP_SECRET when prompted.
  1. In your terminal, run claude mcp add --transport http cloak-biz-scraper {{ server_url }}
  2. Start Claude Code and type /mcp.
  3. It shows the server needs authentication — select it and enter your APP_SECRET.
Signing in is the same secret

Both apps send you here to sign in. Use your APP_SECRET — you never hand either app your licence, proxy, or Notion details.

What you can ask it to do

Once it’s connected, you just talk to it in plain English. A few things to try:

Browse sites that block bots Uses your Evomi proxy for a residential exit IP; without one it uses this server’s datacenter IP, which listing sites may block. A residential proxy needs a CloakBrowser Pro licence — the public build can’t sign in to one.

Open a cloaked browser and drive any site — undetected.

  • “Open a browser, go to this listing, and tell me the asking price and cash flow.”
  • “Click into the third result and summarise the financials.”
Scrape a page of search results

Point it at a search-results page and get every listing back — title, location, price, cash flow, and link.

  • “Scrape this BizBuySell search:” — paste the URL from your address bar after applying your filters.
  • “Search BizBuySell for California businesses under $1M cash flow and scrape the results.”
…and keep them in Notion Needs Notion connected, with a database picked in Settings first.
  • “Scrape that search and add any new ones to my Notion, skipping duplicates.”
  • “Save this listing’s full page to my Notion:” — paste a listing URL.
Get a CDP URL for your own tools Runs through your proxy when configured. The browser stays up ~15 min idle (60 min max); ask again for a fresh URL to reconnect. developers

Launch a cloaked browser and get a CDP URL to drive from Playwright, Claude Code, Codex, or agent-browser.

  • “Launch a browser and give me the CDP URL.”
  • Then point Playwright (connectOverCDP) or agent-browser at that URL and drive it yourself.
