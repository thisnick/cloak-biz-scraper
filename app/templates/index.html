{# The guidance from the plan, verbatim in spirit: the ceiling here is cost, not memory, and the honest framing is that it's cheap because it sleeps. #}

Each running browser uses roughly 0.5–1 GB. You're billed on actual usage (about $10/GB per month), and because the service sleeps when idle, you only pay while a sweep is actually running — a 3-minute sweep with 4 browsers costs pennies. Raise this for faster multi-page sweeps; lower it if you want a tighter cap.

{# ── Security ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Deployment secret

The secret you signed in with. Changing it here takes effect immediately and is remembered on this server's disk — it stays in force even if the APP_SECRET variable in Railway still says something else.

{{ banner('secret') }}

At least 16 characters — this is the only thing standing between the internet and your browsers, proxy, and Notion token. Signs out every other session.
Forgotten it? In Railway, set APP_SECRET to a new value and add APP_SECRET_RESET=true, then redeploy. The next start adopts the new value. Your settings survive — they aren't encrypted with this secret.

Settings are stored encrypted on this server's disk. Anyone with access to the disk can read them regardless — the encryption guards against a stray backup or snapshot, not against someone who already has the volume.
